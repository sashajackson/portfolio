Bio

I was born and raised in New Orleans, Louisiana. At 18, I joined the United States Army where I served 7 years. After the military, I began to teach myself computer programming. First I learned HTML and CSS, then began learning JavaScript. Since beginning to learn computer programming, I have actively participated in the tech community by contributing to forums like Stack Overflow and Medium. I am currently a Jr. at Herzing University New Orleans studying Computer Programming. After I receive my Bachelors of Science, I aspire to attend graduate school and earn a Masters in Computer Science. Aside from educational and career goals, I would like to one day open a Non-profit organization that teaches young adults computer programming for free.
